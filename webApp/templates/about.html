This site was created to support the National Science Foundation Secure and Trustworthy Cyberspace (SaTC) Principal Investigators' Meeting, 5-7 January 2015.

The project award data is from the NSF's public records.

Contributions

Corey Ames (University of Virginia undergraduate student) developed the text analysis and topic modeling code for building models from researcher's abstracts.

Weilin Xu (University of Virginia PhD Student) developed the research matching algorithms and graphing code (using D3).

Yanjun Qi (University of Virginia) provided machine learning expertise.

David Evans (University of Virginia) led the project and did most of the web-hacking, building on a site created by Robert Michaels (University of Virginia undergraduate student).
